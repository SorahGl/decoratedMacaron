The Decorated Macaron is a kosher pastry shop specializing in custom decorated, gluten-free French macarons along with other elegant pastries. Our macarons are handcrafted and made with the finest ingredients. We offer a variety of flavors and designs to suit your needs. Whether you are looking for a simple monogram or an elegant flower vine, we have you covered. Our macarons are perfect for parties, birthdays, Bar/Bas Mitzvahs, and any other special occasion. We take pride in our work and strive to make every order special. We look forward to working with you!
